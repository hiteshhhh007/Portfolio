We spend nearly a third of our lives sleeping, and it’s for a good reason. Here's why sleep is so vital for our health:

### Benefits of Sleep

- **Physical Health**: During sleep, the body heals and repairs itself.
- **Mental Health**: Sleep helps regulate mood, reduces stress, and boosts cognitive function.
- **Improved Focus**: Better sleep leads to better productivity and concentration.
